# Roles and Responsibilities

## Executive Leadership
- Approves the cybersecurity budget.
- Reviews high-risk items and major incidents.
- Signs off on key policies and governance documents.

## Security Lead
- Manages the cybersecurity program.
- Oversees vulnerability management, monitoring, and incident response.
- Ensures implementation of NIST CSF and CIS Controls.
- Leads policy updates and annual reviews.

## IT Administrator
- Implements and maintains technical controls.
- Manages user accounts, systems, and networks.
- Applies patches, monitors logs, and enforces configurations.

## Compliance Manager
- Maintains documentation and evidence.
- Coordinates audits.
- Tracks compliance status through the checklist dashboard.

## Department Managers
- Ensure team members follow security policies.
- Approve access requests.
- Support security awareness and training activities.

## All Employees
- Follow company security policies.
- Complete required training.
- Report suspicious activity or potential incidents.
