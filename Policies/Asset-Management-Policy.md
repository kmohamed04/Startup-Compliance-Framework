# Asset Management Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines how the company identifies, tracks, and manages all information assets throughout their lifecycle. The goal is to ensure assets are protected, monitored, and properly handled from acquisition to disposal.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:

- All company-owned devices (laptops, phones, tablets)  
- All cloud services and SaaS applications  
- All data assets stored or processed by the company  
- All employees, contractors, and interns who use company assets  

## 3. Asset Inventory Requirements
The company must maintain a centralized inventory including:

- Asset type (hardware, software, data)  
- Owner or custodian  
- Serial number or unique identifier  
- Location  
- Status (active, retired, disposed)  
- Sensitivity classification (Confidential, Internal, Public)

## 4. Hardware Asset Management

### Acquisition
- New hardware must be procured through approved vendors.  
- IT Administrator updates the asset inventory upon receipt.

### Usage
- Devices must be used only for business purposes.  
- Users must not install unauthorized software.

### Tracking
- All laptops and mobile devices must have device management agents installed.  
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported immediately.

### Retirement
- Devices must be wiped using approved secure deletion methods before disposal.  
- Decommissioned assets must be marked as “retired” in the inventory.

## 5. Software Asset Management
- Only authorized software may be installed.  
- All software must be licensed and approved by IT.  
- Unsupported or outdated applications must be removed.  
- Cloud applications must be tracked in the SaaS inventory list.

## 6. Data Asset Management
Data must be classified and handled according to the Data Classification Policy.

Data owners must:
- Maintain data accuracy and integrity  
- Approve access based on business needs  
- Ensure proper storage locations (e.g., Google Workspace)  

## 7. Roles and Responsibilities

### IT Administrator
- Maintains hardware and software inventory  
- Ensures devices are properly configured and managed  
- Handles device provisioning and decommissioning

### Security Lead
- Ensures data classification and protection requirements are followed  
- Performs periodic asset audits

### Department Managers
- Ensure team assets are accounted for  
- Report any untracked or unauthorized devices

### Employees
- Use assets responsibly  
- Report lost, stolen, or malfunctioning devices immediately  

## 8. Asset Audits
- Quarterly audits must be performed to verify accuracy of the asset inventory.  
- Any discrepancies must be resolved within 10 business days.  
- Unauthorized devices must be removed from the network.

## 9. Enforcement
Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, loss of device privileges, or termination for severe offenses.
