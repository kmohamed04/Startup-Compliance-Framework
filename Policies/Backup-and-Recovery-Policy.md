# Backup and Recovery Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines how the company performs data backups, stores backup files, and restores systems after failures or security incidents. The goal is to ensure business continuity and protect critical data.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:

- All company systems, cloud services, and applications  
- All data classified as Confidential or Internal  
- All employees and contractors responsible for system administration  

## 3. Backup Requirements

### 3.1 Backup Frequency
- Critical business data must be backed up **daily**.  
- System configurations, logs, and app data must be backed up **weekly**.  
- SaaS data (Google Workspace, CRM, project tools) must follow vendor backup options.

### 3.2 Backup Types
- **Full Backups:** taken monthly  
- **Incremental Backups:** taken daily  
- **Configuration Backups:** taken after major system or network
